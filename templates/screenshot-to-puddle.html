Get Puddle link from game screenshot

I don't blame you for not wanting to type it out, it's really boring. Either way, it's good to know what's expected in your screenshot.

A game screenshot should:

  • Be directly captured from the game - This means that if you try and take a picture of a screen with a camera, the chances are it's not going to work (unless you're incredibly good at alignment and your camera gets no glare from the screen).
    • For those on PC, CTRL+ALT+PrtScn will automatically only get a capture of the currently focused application, which is ideal for this
      • There are quite a few websites that will let you download screenshots from the clipboard to a file, such as this one
    • Console screenshots should also work, but you also need to get them somewhere that lets you upload it here
  • Contain the whole game window
    • For many reasons, the website will look specifically where the ID will be in context of the whole game. Cropping the R-Code out and uploading it will not work.
